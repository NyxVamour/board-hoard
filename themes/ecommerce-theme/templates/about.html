About

Welcome to Board Hoard, where nerdy passions come to life! We’re a team of devoted enthusiasts who live and breathe all things D&D, Warhammer, Magic: The Gathering, and board games of every kind. Our dream was to create a space where fans like us could roll dice, paint minis, and dive into epic card duels—all under one roof. Whether you're a seasoned adventurer or a curious beginner, you'll find a warm, welcoming community here to share in the fun and forge unforgettable memories.

But we’re more than just a gaming space. At Board Hoard, we’ve stocked our shelves with carefully chosen treasures for your favorite hobbies. From tabletop essentials and miniatures to unique collectibles, we’re here to help you level up your nerdy pursuits. We believe in creating a hub where passion meets play, and camaraderie thrives. Come for the games, stay for the friendships—your next adventure starts here!
